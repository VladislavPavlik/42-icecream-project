Ingredients:
160 ml of milk;
3 egg yolks;
150 g sugar;
500 g whipping cream;
some vanilla extract.

Preparation
Heat milk over moderate heat, but do not boil. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar separately. While whisking, pour in the hot milk in a thin stream. The sugar should be completely dissolved.
Pour the mixture into a saucepan and, stirring constantly with a spatula, bring until lightly thickened over medium heat. Do not boil the mixture or overheat it. Run your finger over the shoulder blade: if a clear groove remains, remove the pan from heat.
Place the pot in a container of ice water and, stirring constantly, cool to room temperature. Beat the cold cream with a mixer until creamy.
Pour the milk and egg mixture into the cream through a sieve. Add vanilla extract and stir until smooth. Put the mixture in a wide dish, cover with a lid or plastic wrap and put in the freezer for 3-4 hours. Stir the mixture about every hour until smooth.
